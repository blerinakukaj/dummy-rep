# Customer Notes — CollabDocs Real-time Editor

**Period:** October 15 – November 1, 2025
**Compiled by:** Customer Success Team
**Context:** Post-competitor-launch escalation

---

## Summary

Since Competitor X launched live co-editing on October 15, three enterprise customers have formally escalated with churn risk language. NPS for the collaboration workflow dropped 12 points (from 44 to 32) in the October pulse survey. Competitor mention rate in support tickets and NPS verbatims has risen from under 5% to 28% in three weeks.

---

## Enterprise Escalations

### Acme Corp — ARR: $180K — 200 active users

**CS Owner:** Jamie Reyes
**Escalation Date:** 2025-10-21
**Verbatim from procurement lead:**
> "We evaluated CollabDocs and Competitor X side by side this quarter. Your document locking model is a dealbreaker for our legal and contracts team — we have four people who need to comment and edit simultaneously on the same brief. Competitor X now does this natively. We are giving you 60 days to show a real-time editing roadmap before we make a switch decision."

**Account Status:** At risk. Renewal in January 2026.

---

### Vertex Partners — ARR: $140K — 85 active users

**CS Owner:** Priya Nair
**Escalation Date:** 2025-10-24
**Verbatim from VP of Operations:**
> "We use CollabDocs for our weekly strategy documents. Every Monday we have five people who need to edit the same doc simultaneously and right now only one person can type at a time. It's 2025 — this should be solved. We saw the Competitor X demo. We need this."

**Account Status:** Evaluating alternatives. Trial of Competitor X already started.

---

### NordicBank — ARR: $100K — 120 active users

**CS Owner:** Tobias Lindqvist
**Escalation Date:** 2025-10-28
**Verbatim from IT Director:**
> "Our compliance team flags every tool switch, so we would prefer to stay with CollabDocs. But the locking behavior creates real workflow problems for our audit document reviews. We have sent internal escalations asking to evaluate alternatives. If you can commit to a Q1 2026 delivery of co-editing we will stay."

**Account Status:** Willing to stay if roadmap commitment given by end of November.

## General Customer Feedback (NPS Survey — October 2025)

**NPS Score (collaboration workflow):** 32 (down from 44 in July 2025)

### Selected Verbatims from Detractors

> "The document locking is a dealbreaker. My whole team uses Slack to tell each other 'I'm done editing' before someone else can work on it. That's embarrassing for a document tool."
— Enterprise user, 50-person company

> "Competitor X just launched live editing. We're strongly considering switching unless CollabDocs catches up fast."
— SMB user, 12-person team

> "I left a document open on my laptop and went to a meeting. My colleague couldn't edit for two hours. This is not acceptable."
— Enterprise user, financial services

> "Real-time collaboration is table stakes now. I don't understand why CollabDocs still has a locking model."
— SMB user, consulting firm

> "Mobile editing is practically unusable for collaboration. You can't tell if anyone else is in the doc."
— Mid-market user, marketing agency

### Selected Verbatims from Passives/Promoters

> "Love the formatting tools and version history. Just fix the locking and I'd give 10/10."
— Enterprise user

> "CollabDocs is our primary doc tool. We just wish we could edit together in real time like Google Docs."
— SMB user
