# Customer Support Notes — SearchBoost Query Engine

**Period:** October 29 – November 4, 2025
**Compiled by:** Customer Success Team
**Trigger:** Post-deploy v2.4.1 support spike

---

## Summary

Support ticket volume increased 3x in the 6 days following the v2.4.1 deployment. The vast majority of complaints fall into two categories: irrelevant search results and slow search on mobile. A smaller but growing segment reports broken autocomplete suggestions leading to dead-end pages.

---

## Aggregate Patterns

| Theme                          | Ticket Count | % of Total |
|-------------------------------|-------------|------------|
| Irrelevant / degraded results  | 47          | 38%        |
| Slow search on mobile          | 32          | 26%        |
| Stale / broken autocomplete    | 21          | 17%        |
| Image search not working       | 14          | 11%        |
| Filter not working             | 9           | 7%         |

**Total tickets in period:** 123 (vs. 41 in the equivalent prior 6-day period)

---

## Customer Sentiment

- **Churning / threatening to leave:** 8 enterprise accounts, 3 SMB accounts
- **Explicitly praising v2.4.0 and asking to revert:** 19 tickets
- **Reporting the issue as a bug (not a feature change):** 94% of tickets

---

## Affected Segments

- **Anonymous users:** Disproportionately affected based on ticket language ("not logged in," "just browsing")
- **Mobile web users:** Explicitly cited in 26% of tickets
- **Power users / navigational queries:** Mentioned in 12% of tickets as specifically degraded
