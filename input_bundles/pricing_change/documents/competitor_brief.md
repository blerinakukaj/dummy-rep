# Competitive Brief — CloudMetrics Pricing

## Competitor A: DataDogLite
- **Strengths:** Already consumption-based pricing, transparent cost calculator, generous free tier (50K metrics/month). Strong developer community.
- **Weaknesses:** Costs escalate rapidly at scale (known "bill shock" complaints on social media), limited enterprise support, no custom pricing.
- **Recent moves:** Introduced committed-use discounts (pay upfront for 20% savings) in Q3 2025. Customer satisfaction around pricing is mixed.

## Competitor B: MetricStream
- **Strengths:** Flat-rate pricing that enterprise customers love for budget predictability. All-inclusive tiers with no usage limits.
- **Weaknesses:** Over-provisioned for small customers, no usage-efficient option. Losing startup segment to pay-as-you-go competitors.
- **Recent moves:** Testing a hybrid model in beta: flat base + overage charges above tier limits. Early feedback is negative — perceived as worst of both worlds.
