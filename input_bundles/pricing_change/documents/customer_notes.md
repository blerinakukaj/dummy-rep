# Customer Notes — CloudMetrics Pricing Change

## Interview 1: Alex P. (Startup CTO, Current $49/month plan)
Alex ingests fewer than 10,000 metrics per month and runs about 50 dashboard queries daily. Under the new pricing model, his bill would drop to approximately $22/month. "I love the idea of paying for what I use — the flat rate always felt like I was subsidizing bigger customers." However, he wants clear visibility into usage with no surprise charges.

## Interview 2: Linda W. (Director of Platform, Current $199/month plan)
Linda's team ingests 2.5 million metrics per month and runs complex queries across 40 dashboards. Under the new pricing model, her estimated cost would jump to approximately $480/month. "A 140% price increase is going to be a hard conversation with finance. I need at least 6 months notice and a migration path." She wants spending caps and a cost simulator before committing.

## Interview 3: Tom H. (VP Engineering, Enterprise Account, Custom Pricing)
Tom's organization has a custom $15K/month contract negotiated two years ago. His usage has grown 3x since then. "We're actually underpaying relative to usage, so consumption-based pricing might work in our favor long-term, but we need the price lock guarantee during the transition period." He asked for detailed usage reporting and the ability to set per-team budgets within the organization.
