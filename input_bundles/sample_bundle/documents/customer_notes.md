# Customer Interview Notes — SmartNotify

## Interview 1: Sarah K. (Power User, Enterprise Account)
Sarah uses SmartNotify across three teams and receives an average of 45 notifications per day. She described the experience as "drowning in noise" and said she has resorted to muting entire channels just to stay focused. Her biggest frustration is that transactional alerts (e.g., deployment completions) carry the same visual weight as critical incidents. She would pay more for a tier that offers intelligent filtering. When asked about privacy, she stated she would be comfortable sharing engagement data if it meant fewer irrelevant notifications, but would want to see exactly what data is collected.

## Interview 2: Marcus T. (Casual User, Startup Plan)
Marcus only uses SmartNotify for his 5-person team and checks it a few times a day. He finds the notification volume manageable but says the mobile experience is "broken" — he frequently misses push notifications on his Pixel 8 (Android 14). He mentioned he almost switched to Competitor A's product last month because their mobile reliability is better. He likes the simplicity of SmartNotify but wants digest-style summaries instead of individual pings for non-urgent updates.

## Interview 3: Priya R. (Admin, Mid-Market Account)
Priya manages SmartNotify for a 200-person organization. Her top request is granular notification preferences — she needs to configure defaults per team and let individuals override. She mentioned compliance concerns around notification data retention, asking "how long do you store read receipts and click data?" She also raised accessibility issues: several team members using screen readers have difficulty navigating the notification center, and the color-only priority indicators are problematic for colorblind users.
