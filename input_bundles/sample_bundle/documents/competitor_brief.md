# Competitive Brief — SmartNotify

## Competitor A: PushFlow
- **Strengths:** Excellent mobile reliability (99.7% delivery rate), real-time delivery analytics dashboard, built-in A/B testing for notification copy. Recently launched AI-powered send-time optimization.
- **Weaknesses:** No notification batching/digest feature, limited customization for enterprise, pricing is 40% higher than SmartNotify at scale.
- **Recent moves:** Launched "Smart Timing" feature in Q3 2025 that uses ML to pick optimal delivery windows per user. Early reviews are positive — 18% improvement in click-through rates reported.

## Competitor B: AlertHub
- **Strengths:** Strong enterprise feature set with granular role-based notification policies, SOC 2 certified, WCAG AA compliant notification center. Supports 12 languages out of the box.
- **Weaknesses:** Slow to innovate — no ML features, clunky UI that feels dated, onboarding takes 2-3 weeks for mid-market accounts.
- **Recent moves:** Acquired a small analytics startup in Q4 2025, signaling intent to build metrics/instrumentation features. Enterprise customers report high satisfaction but startup/SMB churn is increasing.

## Competitor C: Notifai
- **Strengths:** AI-native product built from scratch with ML prioritization at the core. Impressive demo showing automatic categorization and priority scoring.
- **Weaknesses:** Very early stage (launched 6 months ago), limited integrations, no enterprise features, reliability concerns (reported 3 outages in the last quarter).
- **Recent moves:** Raised Series A, aggressively hiring ML engineers. Positioning as "the AI-first notification platform." Could become a serious threat in 12-18 months if they solve reliability.
