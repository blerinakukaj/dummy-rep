# Competitive Brief — LearnPath Accessibility

## Competitor A: EduAccess LMS
- **Strengths:** WCAG 2.1 AA fully conformant, published VPAT updated quarterly, dedicated accessibility team of 6 engineers. ARIA patterns are exemplary — frequently cited in accessibility blog posts as best practice. Keyboard navigation covers 100% of features.
- **Weaknesses:** Limited feature set outside of core LMS, no AI features, dated visual design. Higher pricing ($12/user/month vs LearnPath's $8).
- **Recent moves:** Achieved WCAG 2.2 AA conformance in Q3 2025, first LMS to do so. Won 3 major government contracts citing accessibility as the deciding factor.

## Competitor B: SkillForge
- **Strengths:** Modern UI, AI-powered personalized learning paths, strong mobile experience, competitive pricing.
- **Weaknesses:** Similar accessibility gaps to LearnPath — no VPAT, multiple WCAG failures. Lost a $600K government deal last quarter due to accessibility non-compliance.
- **Recent moves:** Announced an "accessibility initiative" in Q4 2025 but no timeline or VPAT commitment yet. Community forums show frustrated users with disabilities.
